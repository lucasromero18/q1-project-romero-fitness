THE FUTURE OF FITNESS HAS ARRIVED

Strength & Resistance Training

Accumulating strength mentally and physically are both crucial when exercise comes into play. Burning fat AND building muscle? Sounds like the best of both worlds to us over at Romero Fitness. Along with age, Strength / Resistance Training helps prevent the natural loss of lean muscle mass with aging. Close to the age of 30, is when humans begin to experience muscle mass loss (generally 3-5% per year) which is something we dont want! Learn how to manipulate the muscles and emphasize growth everyday, while having an exciting experience every session!

Cardiovascular Training

In relation to walking, running, hiking, biking and other physical activity done throughout the day, comes cardiovascular training in the gym. Working on targeting improved heart health, an increased metabolism, improving recovery ability, managing disease prevention (etc.) are the beautiful benefits that come along with cardiovascular training. HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) training and advanced tempo training are both offered as well. Learn how to incorporate cardiovascular training into your strength training as efficiently as possible, come on in to make a difference and feel the absolute best you can!

Self Myofascial Release

Along with strength training and cardiovascular training, comes executing the process properly. Preventing injury, and reducing the amount of soreness one may experience through endorphin release, improved range of motion, improved neuromuscular efficiency, and helping promote repair and growth of muscle are all also crucial throughout the duratiuon of changing your body physically. We make sure that the proper steps are taken to help you reach your goal!
