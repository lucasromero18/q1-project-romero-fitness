What exactly is BMI?

"Body Mass Index" is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height, weight and age. BMI determines whether a person is underweight, overweight, obese or a healthy weight for their height.

You can test your BMI via the hand held BMI machine, Rice Lake D1000-3 Full Body Composition Analyzer (comparing water, muscle, and body fat ratios), and the BMI pinch test (measured by calipers) to determine the fat layer thickness.
